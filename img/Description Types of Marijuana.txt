OG Shark
 is a rare hybrid marijuana strain that is primarily found in Canada. OG Shark is beloved for its high potency, earthy aroma and therapeutic qualities. The effects of this strain are sedating and relaxing. Medical marijuana patients choose OG Shark to relieve symptoms associated with nausea, pain and insomnia. Growers say this strain grows best in a dry, outdoor climate.

Jack Herer 
is a slightly sativa-dominant hybrid that provides a clear-headed, cerebral high coupled with a strong energetic indica buzz at the same time. The high experienced from the strain is euphoric and energetic, on a level that goes beyond what most sativas are capable of.


Jean Guy
 is a famous sativa strain from the Montreal Compassion Club and was the first ever cannabis strain to be featured in the New York times. The Jean Guy is a sativa dominant cross. It grows quickly, producing thick lateral branches and heavy colas. It is a finicky plant best suited to advanced growers.

Blue dream 
is another hybrid strain of weed that is slightly sativa-dominant. It gives an energetic cerebral high that can increase motivation and heighten focus. Some people describe it as having relaxing and pain-relieving effects. 

Its name comes from the fact that the origin of the strain is unknown, making it like a dream. It has a sweet taste that some describe as similar to blueberries and sugar.

People who have chronic fatigue, depression or a lack of appetite have described blue dream as having therapeutic effects that improve these conditions. People have also described it as relieving chronic pain and migraines.
